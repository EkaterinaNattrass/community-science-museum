bioloGy And Medicine

Come visit our unique space called 'The wonders of Medicine' which shows how modern medicine has been developing and helping people improve their quality of life.

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of our world.

Over a series of exhibits we explore the history of medicine and take a look at some major breakthroughs including the discovery of penicillin and the first heart transplant.

cosMoloGy

Our fantastic exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us', explores the night sky and what we can learn about the universe around us.

We'll locate various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo's telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

eVolution

For centuries, philosophers and scientists have wrestled with the question of our origins. Since Darwin proposed his theory of evolution we have had a framework for exploring and understanding our place in this world.

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time.

You can also see a few extinct species, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

robotics And Ai

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future.

From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here.

You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.
